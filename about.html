Who we are at `The Zen Pup`

` The Zen Pup` is a dog grooming salon that caters to our lovely customers here in Albuquerque. Its full grooming service includes anal gland expression, baths, ear cleaning, hair cuts, and nail trimming. This business also offers a self-wash service and provides aprons, blueberry facials, brushes, colognes, and professional dryers. As a former volunteer for a local humane animal organization, owner Jonathan has been able to foster animals who were in need of medical care alongside his partner, Matt.
